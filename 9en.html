General information about Belarus

Belarus Belarus (officially named the Republic of Belarus) is a presidential republic in Eastern Europe with a population of 9,409,000 people and a territory of 207,595 sq. km.

The capital – Minsk
Currency – Belarusian ruble Br (BYN)
Has borders with Ukraine, Poland, Lithuania, Latvia and Russia.
Official language – Russian, Belarusian

The most common form of doing business in Belarus is the creation of companies. The conditions for creating companies for national Belarusian and foreign investors, as well as the conditions of such companies operating their activities are the same.

Our specialists are ready to provide you with consulting services on opening companies in RB, accompanying during the entire period of registration of your business.

There are three most common forms of companies in Belarus:

  • Sole proprietorship.
  • Limited liability company (LLC).
  • Open joint-stock company (JSC) and closed joint-stock company (CJSC).

The opening stages of the LLC in Belarus:

To register a company in the Republic of Belarus you need to go through the following procedures:

  • APPROVAL OF THE NAME OF THE LLC

    The first step is to decide what your organization will be called.

  • LEGAL ADDRESS LTD.

    The legal address of the organization (also – the location) – is determined by the location of the head of the company (administrative-territorial unit, settlement, as well as a house, apartment or other premises, if they are available). In addition, when opening an LLC, there are options to choose a legal address for a minimum fee, and sometimes, taking into account the type of activity of the organization – and free of charge.

  • DETERMINE THE SIZE OF THE STATUTORY FUND LTD.

    Belarus's legislation does not currently require a minimum size of a company's statutory fund as a limited liability company, so you can form a statutory fund of any amount. The charter fund must be formed within 12 months of the state registration of the LLC.

  • PREPARE THE FOUNDING DOCUMENTS

    Charter LLC, the founding agreement, as well as other documents for filing with the registration authority, not contrary to the law.

  • PAY THE STATE FEE

    - 29 Belarusian rubles (1 basic value).

  • REGISTER AN LLC WITH A REGISTRATION AUTHORITY.

    Registration of the LLC takes place on the day of filing the full package of documents to the registration authority. From that moment on, the LLC is considered to be created.

  • FOR THE FULL OPERATION OF YOUR NEW COMPANY REMAINS:

    - to apply for the job of the head of the LLC;
    - to make a seal;
    - open a checking account at the bank.
