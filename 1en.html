What is SEO? Why does modern business need it?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is a set of measures and actions by which optimizers can find and eliminate technical errors on the site, by improving the readability of the resource for search engines.
SEO is the foundation on which business promotion in social networks is based.

Through this process, becomes easier to find the necessary information for the user that will best meet his request to Google, Yandex, Yahoo and other search engines. For the business, SEO optimization also plays an important role, as it makes possible to bring even a new site to the top and make it more visited, which means attracting new customers. In addition, in the process of internal optimization, the site becomes more user-friendly, therefore, it leaves a pleasant impression and makes you want to return to the site again, make another purchase and advise it to your colleagues and friends.
