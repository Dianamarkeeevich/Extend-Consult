The positioning of the business contributes to the formation of a brand value, as well as reinforces the positive impression of the brand in the memory of consumers.

The main role of positioning is to allocate the company among others, producing identical goods or services.

The company “Extend Consult” provides high-quality services in matters of
business positioning in the market. Among them:

  • Site development;
  • Mobile app design;
  • Redesign of the current site;
  • Formulating company's corporate style (logo, business cards, booklets, banners on social networks, etc.)

What is a corporate identity?

Branding is one of the tools of brand formation and at the same time an element of branding.

The main elements of the corporate style:

  • Logo;
  • Slogan;
  • Branded advertising block;
  • Branded font;
  • Branded colour;
  • Layout scheme;
  • Modular mesh.

All of the above elements are necessarily presented on the company's website.
Today, more and more customers search for goods and services on the Internet, so the presence of the site is extremely important for any business. In fact, it is the cheapest way of advertising business on the Internet. The Internet resource allows you to attract customers both from your city or country, and more distant regions.

What tasks does the presence of the site can solve?

  • First of all, the site carries out the main goal of any business – the realization of goods and services.
  • Secondly, the creation of the site allows you to keep in touch with the customers in 24/7 mode.

    • Due to this, the company has the opportunity to study the target audience, their needs, the reaction to the product and get a full feedback.

A website is the face of a business on the Internet. It conveys the mood, mission, and core principles of the company. A well-designed website is able to find new customers on its own and answer the most frequently asked questions, without resorting to the help of employees.
