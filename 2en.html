What is an investment?

Investment is a holding of available untapped cash in order to generate income in the future. Investing helps not only to save money, but also to increase it.

Why is it necessary to attract investment in the project?

In today's reality, the market is developing and transforming very quickly, because of this, for a new product that requires significant financial and time costs, the period of time its relevance is very limited. Here can come to the aid of investments that will contribute not only to the timely launch of the product, but also to the preservation of a niche in the market for the company. Also, in order to be successful in a competitive environment, the business needs to develop and grow, otherwise it risks to lose its position. Development always requires financial investment. The company can use its own money or, if it’s not enough, attract investments.

How to attract investment?

There are several ways to raise money. Some of them:

  • Thematic events: business conferences, forums, roundtables, exhibitions, fairs, presentations, etc.;
  • Appeal to special funds that support small and medium-sized businesses;
  • Collaboration with organizations that help start-up entrepreneurs to implement new projects at all stages of their early stages;
  • Appeal to agencies providing specialized assistance in finding an investor;
  • Posting ads on the thematic portals on the Internet;
  • . Using online services to showcase project presentations to potential investors and sending out investment applications.

It is worth noting that attracting investment in the project is not enough. It is very important to use them correctly in order to form a strong and long-term relationship with the investor. Our specialists will help you to use the raised funds and create a favorable image of your company in the eyes of investors.
