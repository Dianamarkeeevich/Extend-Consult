What is a franchise?

A franchise – is a transfer of the rights to use the brand and brand under certain conditions to a second party. In other words, a franchise can be perceived as a long-term lease of a successful business idea with the right to use a well-known brand.

Choosing in favor of franchising at the beginning of your own business allows you to strongly minimize the risks for the entrepreneur.

The main advantages of buying a franchise:

  • Brand name. The main advantage of the franchise is that the hardest work has already done.
  • Established communication with suppliers The franchisor provides a list of customers, partners and suppliers for free.
  • Established channels of raw materials and equipment supply, ready-made standards of the establishment of the outlet and so on. All this, as well as the rules of internal order have already been prescribed by the franchisor under the terms of the contract.
  • Support from the franchisor. The company-organizer is no less than the Frenchman is interested in the success of his case.
  • Lower advertising costs. When buying a franchise, the buyer receives a franchise package, which includes all the information and products necessary to promote the business.
  • Low level of competition. The franchisor seeks to expand the service area and not to organize businesses in one territory.

To buy a franchise, it is necessary to comply not only with the internal rules of the brand, but also with the requirements of the state, on the territory of which it was created and carries out its activities.

What should be considered in the process of buying a franchise in RB territory?

The main act of regulation franchising relations in Belarus is the Civil Code of the Republic of Belarus. According to it, the franchising contract provides for the use of the specified license complex to a certain extent (with the establishment of a minimum and/or maximum use) indicating or without specifying the territory of their use in relation to certain types of business activities.

In order to register a franchise in Belarus, you must submit the following documents to the registration authority:

  • Application for registration of the contract;
  • Contract in three copies (two copies – originals, the third copy – a certified copy);
  • Document confirming payment of fee for registration of the contract;
  • Power of attorney.

Registration of franchising contracts is carried out within one month of the date of receipt of the correct documents to the patent authority.
